    Bill of materials¶

    PTFE Tube lengths

    I have split the lengths into two, the drive side and the hotend side. You need to sum the two lenghts from this and the drive BOM you'd be using. (preferrably get more PTFE)

    Do not cut the PTFE into two pieces

    Item Quantity Name Printable
    1 1.0 A189 no
    2 1.0 bmo_face yes
    3 1.0 bmo_support yes
    4 6.0 M3 Hex nut no
    5 6.0 DIN 912 M3 25 mm no
    6 2.0 DIN 912 M3 8 mm no
    7 1.0 4010 FAN no
    8 1.0 DIN 912 M3 6 mm no
    9 1.0 PTFE 33.4mm no
